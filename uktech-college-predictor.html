Understanding UKTECH / UTU Counselling Process & Choice Filling

Engineering admissions in Uttarakhand state colleges are administered by Veer Madho Singh Bhandari Uttarakhand Technical University (UTU / UKTECH), Dehradun. UKTECH conducts centralized online counselling rounds based on JEE Main overall ranks. During option filling, candidates enter their preferred combinations of colleges and branches. Allotment occurs in rank order; once a matching preference is found, it is allocated to the candidate.

Uttarakhand B.Tech Fee Structure & Fee Concessions

  • Government & UTU Constituent Colleges (GBPIET, THDC-IHET, BTKIT, IT Gopeshwar): Subsidized tuition fees ranging around Rs 25,000 to Rs 45,000 per year.
  • Tuition Fee Waiver (TFW) Scheme: Candidates with family annual income below Rs 8 Lakhs are eligible for 5% reserved supernumerary TFW seats with 100% tuition fee exemption in government and private engineering colleges.
  • SC/ST/OBC Post-Matric Scholarship: Reserved category students having family income within government prescribed limits receive tuition fee reimbursement through Uttarakhand Social Welfare Department.

Uttarakhand B.Tech Admissions, Expected Cutoffs & JEE Main Rank Insights

Securing admission into Uttarakhand's top engineering colleges through UKTECH requires a clear understanding of choice-filling rules, category-wise quotas, and previous closing rank trends.

Uttarakhand Reservation & Quotas

UKTECH implements Uttarakhand government's reservation rules:

  • OBC Reservation (14%): Other Backward Classes (Non-Creamy Layer) candidates receive seat reservations and cutoff rank relaxations across state colleges.
  • SC (19%) & ST (4%) Reservations: Scheduled Caste and Scheduled Tribe candidates receive category relaxations and Social Welfare tuition fee waivers.
  • Female Horizontal Quota (30%): Reserved female seats exist within UK-GEN-W, UK-OBC-W, UK-SC-W, and UK-ST-W quotas to support female engineering aspirants.

UKTECH Allocation Factors

Closing ranks vary dynamically during choice filling rounds based on these critical factors:

  • UKTECH Quota Division: 85% seats are reserved for Uttarakhand State Domicile candidates, while 15% seats are open under All India Quota.
  • Choice Filling Rankings: Allotment is strictly based on the order of choices filled by the candidate. If eligible for multiple choices, candidates get the highest preference.
  • TFW 5% Supernumerary Seats: Tuition Fee Waiver seats are allotted to eligible candidates having family income below Rs 8 LPA based on JEE Main merit.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Uttarakhand UKTECH College Predictor and how does it work?
The Uttarakhand UKTECH College Predictor is a free tool that estimates your B.Tech admission chances in top engineering colleges in Uttarakhand (like GBPIET Pauri, THDC-IHET Tehri, BTKIT Dwarahat, IT Gopeshwar, SIT Pithoragarh, WIT Dehradun, COER Roorkee, Tula's Institute). Enter your JEE Main rank, select your reservation category and preferred branch, and the tool compares your rank against previous years official UKTECH / UTU closing ranks to show High, Medium, or Low admission probabilities.
How does UKTECH choice filling work in Uttarakhand?
UKTECH option entry allows candidates to select college-branch combinations in order of priority. UKTECH's allotment engine processes applications in rank order. If a seat is available in your highest choice at your rank, it is allotted to you. Once a choice is allotted, lower preference choices are discarded, making option ordering critical.
Is the predicted seat guaranteed?
No. Actual seat allotments are determined by UKTECH based on the candidate's rank, choices filled by all students, category-wise seat availability, and actual closing cutoffs in the current year. This tool provides indicative assessments based on historical trends.
What is UKTECH and who conducts Uttarakhand B.Tech counselling?
UKTECH (Veer Madho Singh Bhandari Uttarakhand Technical University, UTU Dehradun) is the state technical university that conducts the centralized online B.Tech counselling for state government autonomous, constituent, and affiliated private engineering colleges across Uttarakhand.
What reservation categories are recognized under UKTECH?
UKTECH provides seat reservations for UK-GEN, UK-GEN Women (UK-GEN-W), UK OBC Non-Creamy Layer (UK-OBC & UK-OBC-W), UK Scheduled Caste (UK-SC & UK-SC-W), UK Scheduled Tribe (UK-ST & UK-ST-W), Economically Weaker Section (UK-EWS), Tuition Fee Waiver (UK-TFW), Defense Wards (UK-DEF), Freedom Fighter (UK-FF), and Differently Abled (UK-PWD) quotas.
Does Uttarakhand domicile matter for UKTECH seat allotment?
Yes. 85% of total seats in state engineering colleges are reserved for candidates holding Uttarakhand domicile certificates. The remaining 15% seats are open for All India Quota (AI Quota) candidates from any Indian state.
How does Tuition Fee Waiver (TFW) work under UKTECH?
Under AICTE and UKTECH rules, 5% supernumerary seats in all participating institutions are reserved for Tuition Fee Waiver (TFW). Candidates whose annual family income is below Rs 8 Lakhs are eligible for 100% tuition fee exemption under the TFW category.
Can I edit my choices after locking them in UKTECH?
During the specified choice filling dates, you can modify, add, or delete choices as many times as you like. However, after the final choice locking date, they cannot be modified. Our Choice Basket allows you to reorder and export your preference list prior to official entry.
