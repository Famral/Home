About the UPTAC College Predictor 2026

The UPTAC College Predictor 2026 is a free, data-driven tool built exclusively for Uttar Pradesh B.Tech aspirants. It estimates your admission probability across 25+ AKTU-affiliated engineering colleges — spanning both government and private institutions across Lucknow, Kanpur, Ghaziabad, Greater Noida, Gorakhpur, Jhansi, and other UP cities — by comparing your JEE Main CRL Rank or Class 12th PCM percentage against verified historical UPTAC cutoff data from 2024–2026.

Whether you are a UP domicile candidate seeking Home State Quota seats or an All India category aspirant, the tool precisely adjusts predictions based on your reservation category (General/UR, OBC-NCL, EWS, SC, ST), gender horizontal quota, and TFWS eligibility — giving you the most comprehensive and tailored UPTAC 2026 B.Tech college prediction experience available online.

Dual Score Entry Modes

Enter either your JEE Main Percentile or your Class 12th PCM percentage to get predictions. The tool maps both score types against historical UPTAC cutoff data to deliver accurate college-branch match results for 2026 admissions.

Official Cutoff Database

Built on verified cutoff data covering all 13 major AKTU-affiliated engineering colleges including IET Lucknow, KNIT Sultanpur, BIET Jhansi, JSS Noida, AKGEC, KIET, and more. Cutoffs are maintained for every branch, category (UR/OBC/SC/ST/EWS), domicile, and TFWS quota combination.

Category & Domicile Aware

The predictor applies the correct cutoff adjustments for your reservation category (General, OBC, EWS, SC, ST) and domicile state (Uttar Pradesh Home State Quota vs. All India Category). Female applicants also get gender-based horizontal seat consideration (20% quota) for applicable colleges.

Uttar Pradesh B.Tech Admission & UPTAC 2026 Cutoff Insights

Securing admission into Uttar Pradesh's top engineering colleges under UPTAC requires a clear understanding of the UPTAC 2026 portal guidelines, reservation quota policies, and year-on-year cutoff rank trends. This section provides essential context to help you make informed choices during the official UPTAC choice-filling window.

Top UPTAC Engineering Colleges

Uttar Pradesh has a mix of prestigious government autonomous university departments and high-performing private colleges:

  • IET Lucknow: Premier state government college under UPTAC in UP's capital. CSE/IT cutoffs close under 40,000 CRL. Excellent coding culture and active tech societies.
  • HBTU Kanpur (Harcourt Butler Technical University): One of UP's oldest and most prestigious technical institutions. Strong in Chemical, Polymer, and traditional engineering branches with very affordable government-aided fees.
  • KNIT Sultanpur & BIET Jhansi: Top government-aided institutions with heavily subsidized fees (GAS seats), strong core engineering placements, and large green campuses.
  • MMMUT Gorakhpur (Madan Mohan Malaviya University): A university-level government institution with excellent NAAC accreditation, serving Eastern UP candidates with low fees and strong research culture.
  • JSS Noida, AKGEC & KIET Ghaziabad: Leading private institutions in the Delhi NCR belt with robust placement records, industry lab tie-ups (Bosch, NI, Kuka Robotics), and high-package CSE/IT results.
  • GCET Greater Noida, GL Bajaj, ABES & NIET: Well-established private colleges offering strong placement support at relatively accessible rank cutoffs for students with CRL ranks between 1–4 lakh.
  • REC Colleges (Sonbhadra, Bijnor, Ambedkar Nagar, Kannauj, Azamgarh, Banda): Rajkiya Engineering Colleges offer government brand value with extremely affordable fees (₹50,000–65,000/year) and are great options for candidates with CRL ranks beyond 1.5 lakh.

UPTAC Seat Allocation Factors

Cutoff ranks vary dynamically during counseling. Keep these primary factors in mind:

  • JEE Main CRL AIR Rank: UPTAC uses All India CRL ranks for seat allocation. Category ranks are used as filters under the specific reservation quotas rather than the category-specific merit positions.
  • Preference Checklist: Place your dream colleges at the top of your list. The Choice-Filling Basket on this page is designed to help you construct a structured preference list to copy-paste during official UPTAC registration.
  • TFWS Eligibility: Make sure your income certificate is issued and verified correctly prior to UPTAC counseling, confirming your family's annual income is below ₹8 Lakhs.

UPTAC 2026 Choice Filling Strategy & Document Checklist

A well-prepared choice list and complete documentation are the two most critical factors in securing your preferred seat during UPTAC 2026 counseling rounds.

UPTAC Choice Filling Tips

  • Rank your dream college first: Under UPTAC's allotment algorithm, the system assigns you the highest-preference option for which you qualify. Always put your most desired college at the top.
  • Add both safe and reach options: Include colleges where you have High Chance, Medium Chance, and at least 1–2 Low/Borderline options. A long list maximizes your chances in Round 1 seat allotment.
  • Do not lock a list mid-editing: Keep editing your choice list until the last hour of the choice-filling window. Cutoffs from Round 1 results can inform your Round 2 strategy for upgrades.
  • TFWS candidates: Add TFWS seats above regular UR seats of the same college — TFWS cutoffs may be tighter but fees are zero. Verify income certificate validity before applying.

UPTAC Document Checklist

  • JEE Main 2026 Scorecard (with CRL Rank printed)
  • Class 12th Mark Sheet & Passing Certificate
  • Class 10th Mark Sheet (for DOB & Board verification)
  • UP Domicile / Residence Certificate (issued by competent authority)
  • Category Certificate (OBC-NCL / SC / ST / EWS) — current year issued
  • Income & Asset Certificate (for EWS and TFWS applicants — annual income < ₹8 Lakhs)
  • Passport-size photographs (recent, white background)

Frequently Asked Questions about UPTAC College Predictor 2026

Answers to the most common questions asked by JEE Main aspirants planning for UPTAC 2026 B.Tech admission in Uttar Pradesh.

What is the UPTAC College Predictor and how does it work?
The UPTAC College Predictor is a free tool that estimates your B.Tech admission chances in Uttar Pradesh engineering colleges under UPTAC. Enter your JEE Main CRL Rank or Class 12th PCM percentage, select your category, domicile, and preferred branch, and the tool compares your score against historical UPTAC cutoffs to show likely, safe, and reach colleges.
Which score should I enter — JEE Main CRL Rank or PCM percentage?
UPTAC 2026 uses JEE Main CRL Rank as the primary merit criterion. Enter your JEE Main Common Rank List (CRL) rank for the most accurate predictions. If you have not appeared in JEE Main, you may use the PCM percentage mode which maps board marks to an estimated admission likelihood based on historical data.
Is the predicted seat guaranteed?
No. Allotments are determined by the official UPTAC counseling board based on live registrations and choices locked in the current year. This tool provides indicative predictions based on previous year cutoff trends. Actual cutoffs may vary year-to-year due to changes in applicant pool and seat availability.
What is the TFWS seat category in UPTAC?
TFWS stands for Tuition Fee Waiver Scheme. It provides 5% supernumerary seats for UP-domicile candidates whose annual family income is below ₹8 Lakhs. TFWS seats waive 100% of the tuition fee — the student pays only development and miscellaneous charges. These seats often have slightly higher cutoffs than regular UR seats.
How does the domicile setting affect predictions?
Uttar Pradesh reserves the majority of engineering seats for UP-domicile candidates (Home State Quota). The remaining seats are open to candidates from other states (All India Quota). Cutoffs for All India seats are generally more competitive as competition is national. Select your correct domicile for accurate predictions.
Does the gender setting affect admission chances?
Yes. AKTU institutions reserve 20% horizontal quotas for female candidates. This generally results in slightly relaxed cutoff thresholds (higher ranks allowed) for female aspirants in colleges like IET Lucknow, KNIT Sultanpur, and BIET Jhansi.
Which colleges are covered in the UPTAC College Predictor database?
The UPTAC predictor covers 25+ AKTU-affiliated engineering colleges including IET Lucknow, HBTU Kanpur, KNIT Sultanpur, BIET Jhansi, MMMUT Gorakhpur, AIET Lucknow, JSS Academy Noida, AKGEC Ghaziabad, KIET Ghaziabad, Galgotias College (GCET) Greater Noida, GL Bajaj Greater Noida, ABES Ghaziabad, NIET Greater Noida, PSIT Kanpur, SRMC Lucknow, MPEC Kanpur, RKGIT Ghaziabad, HCE Sonbhadra, Invertis University Bareilly, Galgotias University Greater Noida, REC Bijnor, REC Ambedkar Nagar, REC Kannauj, REC Azamgarh, and REC Banda.
