Frequently Asked Questions

A College Predictor is a free tool that estimates your B.Tech admission chances in engineering colleges based on your entrance exam score (JEE Main percentile or board marks), category, and domicile. It compares your score against historical counseling cutoffs to show likely, safe, and reach colleges — helping you make smarter choices during counseling.

Famral offers free college predictors for four major state engineering counseling processes: REAP (Rajasthan), MP DTE (Madhya Pradesh), HSTES (Haryana), and ACPC (Gujarat). Each predictor is tailored to the respective state's counseling rules, seat matrix, and cutoff data.

JEE Main percentile is accepted as the primary merit criterion for REAP, MP DTE, and HSTES counseling. ACPC (Gujarat) additionally supports GUJCET scores. Each predictor specifies which score to enter for the most accurate results.

No. College Predictors provide indicative estimates based on historical cutoff trends from previous years. Actual seat allotments depend on the live applicant pool, choices locked, and seat availability in the current counseling year. Always cross-check with the official counseling authority before finalizing your choices.
