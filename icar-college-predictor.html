Understanding ICAR UG Counselling & Seat Allotment

Admission to undergraduate courses in Agriculture and Allied Sciences is conducted by the Indian Council of Agricultural Research (ICAR) based on NTA CUET (UG) / ICAR All India Overall Merit Ranks. ICAR conducts centralized online counselling for:

  • 100% Seats in Central Agricultural Universities (CAUs): IARI New Delhi, RPCAU Pusa Bihar, RLBCAU Jhansi, and CAU Imphal.
  • 100% Seats in ICAR Deemed Universities: NDRI Karnal, IVRI Izatnagar, and CIFE Mumbai.
  • 15% - 20% All India Quota (AIQ) Seats: In all State Agricultural Universities (SAUs) like GBPUAT Pantnagar, PAU Ludhiana, CCSHAU Hisar, TNAU Coimbatore, UAS Bangalore, etc.

ICAR Agriculture Admissions, Reservation Quotas & Merit Insights

Securing admission into India's top agricultural universities through ICAR counselling requires a clear understanding of reservation rules, category quotas, and historical closing rank trends.

ICAR Reservation Rules & Quotas

ICAR implements Government of India reservation guidelines for All India Quota seats:

  • OBC-NCL Reservation (27%): Other Backward Classes (Non-Creamy Layer) candidates receive seat reservation across all Central & State Agricultural Universities.
  • SC (15%) & ST (7.5%) Reservations: Scheduled Caste and Scheduled Tribe candidates get significant category rank relaxations and fee concessions.
  • EWS (10%) & PwD (5%): Economically Weaker Section and Persons with Disability quotas are applied as per central guidelines.

ICAR Allocation Factors

Closing ranks vary dynamically during ICAR counselling rounds based on these factors:

  • ICAR AIQ Seat Share: 15%-20% seats in SAUs and 100% seats in CAUs/DUs are allotted strictly through ICAR All India Merit Rank.
  • Choice Filling Ranking: Allotment is processed in sequence of options filled. Prioritize high-demand programs like B.Sc. Agriculture and B.Tech Agri Engg at the top.
  • ICAR NTS Scholarship: Candidates allotted SAUs outside their domicile state qualify for ICAR National Talent Scholarship (NTS) of Rs 3,000/month.

Frequently Asked Questions

What makes this ICAR College Predictor accurate?
Our predictor incorporates official historical ICAR All India closing ranks for 40+ premier agricultural universities across Round 1, Round 2, Round 3, and Mop-Up rounds. It accounts for category multipliers (UR, OBC-NCL, EWS, SC, ST, PwD, UPS), seat quotas (ICAR AIQ vs SAU State Domicile), and specific program cutoffs to deliver high-precision admission probability estimates.
How to convert CUET Score to ICAR All India Rank?
Candidates can use our built-in CUET Score Converter tool above. Entering your total CUET score (out of 600) or NTA percentile calculates an estimated ICAR All India Overall Merit Rank based on score distribution algorithms.
How is ICAR UG seat allocation conducted?
ICAR seat allocation is conducted through centralized online counselling based on NTA CUET (UG) / ICAR All India Overall Merit Ranks. Allotment is carried out for 15%-20% All India Quota seats in State Agricultural Universities (SAUs) and 100% seats in Central Agricultural Universities (CAUs) and ICAR Deemed Universities.
What is the ICAR National Talent Scholarship (NTS)?
The ICAR National Talent Scholarship (NTS) provides Rs 3,000 per month to undergraduate students admitted to a State Agricultural University located outside their home state through ICAR All India Quota counselling.
What is the difference between CAUs, DUs, and SAUs in ICAR?
Central Agricultural Universities (CAUs like RPCAU Pusa, RLBCAU Jhansi, CAU Imphal) and Deemed Universities (DUs like IARI New Delhi, NDRI Karnal) fall under central jurisdiction with 100% seats filled via ICAR counselling. State Agricultural Universities (SAUs like GBPUAT, PAU, TNAU) are managed by state governments and reserve 15%-20% seats for ICAR All India Quota while remaining seats are filled via state entrance/domicile quotas.
Is the predicted seat guaranteed?
No. Actual seat allotments are determined by ICAR during official counselling rounds based on student ranks, choice preferences, category quotas, and current-year closing cutoffs.
Which courses are offered through ICAR UG counselling?
ICAR UG counselling offers B.Sc. (Hons.) Agriculture, B.Sc. (Hons.) Horticulture, B.Sc. (Hons.) Forestry, B.F.Sc. (Fisheries Science), B.Tech. Agricultural Engineering, B.Tech. Dairy Technology, B.Tech. Food Technology, B.Tech. Biotechnology, and B.Sc. (Hons.) Community Science.
