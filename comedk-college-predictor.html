How to Use the COMEDK UGET College Predictor for B.Tech Admissions

Choosing the right Karnataka engineering college and B.Tech branch is a crucial decision. The COMEDK UGET College Predictor simplifies this by leveraging official historical COMEDK counselling data and closing ranks to provide accurate insights into which colleges and branches are within your reach.

Understanding COMEDK UGET Counselling

COMEDK UGET (Under Graduate Entrance Test) is a state-level engineering entrance examination conducted by the Consortium of Medical, Engineering and Dental Colleges of Karnataka for admission to B.Tech programs in 160+ private unaided engineering colleges in Karnataka. COMEDK counselling is conducted online and uses a centralised rank-based seat allotment process. Candidates fill their college and branch preferences, and seats are allotted based on COMEDK rank, availability, and the preference order submitted.

COMEDK Seat Types & Fee Structure

  • COMEDK Merit Seats (65%): Allocated through COMEDK UGET rank-based centralised counselling. Annual tuition fees typically range from Rs 1.2 Lakh to Rs 2.5 Lakh depending on the college tier.
  • Management Quota Seats (35%): Allocated directly by individual institutions. Fees can range from Rs 3 Lakh to Rs 8+ Lakh/year for premium colleges.
  • NRI Quota (5%): Reserved for Non-Resident Indians. Fees are collected in USD/EUR and are significantly higher.
  • SNQ - Horanadu/Gadinadu Kannadiga Quota: A special government quota available to Karnataka domicile students with relaxed fee structures.

Steps to Build Your COMEDK Preference List

  • Step 1 - Enter Your COMEDK Rank: Input your COMEDK UGET 2026 rank. Ranks from 1 to 2,00,000 are supported for predictions across all 160+ participating colleges.
  • Step 2 - Select Seat Type: Choose your seat type (COMEDK merit, Management Quota, or NRI Quota) to get relevant closing rank comparisons.
  • Step 3 - Filter by Branch & Tier: Select your preferred branch and college tier to narrow down results. High Chance are your safe bets, Medium Chance are your targets, Low Chance are your dream colleges.
  • Step 4 - Compare Colleges: Use the Compare button to evaluate up to 3 colleges side-by-side across fees, placement stats, ratings, and campus details.
  • Step 5 - Build & Export Preference List: Add colleges to your basket, reorder them strategically (Dream choices first, Safety last), and export a PDF to use during official COMEDK web counselling.

Frequently Asked Questions - COMEDK UGET College Predictor

Everything you need to know about the COMEDK UGET 2026 exam, counselling process, and how to use this predictor effectively.

What is the COMEDK UGET College Predictor and how does it work?
The COMEDK UGET College Predictor is a free tool that estimates your B.Tech admission chances in 160+ Karnataka private engineering colleges. Enter your COMEDK UGET rank, select your preferred branch, college tier, and seat type. The tool compares your rank against official COMEDK closing ranks from 2024 and 2025 and classifies each college as High Chance, Medium Chance, or Low/Borderline Chance.
How accurate are the COMEDK cutoff predictions?
Predictions are based on official COMEDK UGET closing ranks from the last 2 counselling rounds. Cutoffs typically shift by 5-15% year-on-year depending on the total number of applicants, seat availability, and the popularity of branches. Always cross-check with the official COMEDK website (comedk.org) before making final decisions.
Does COMEDK UGET have any category-based reservation?
COMEDK merit seats (65% of total intake) are purely rank-based with no SC/ST/OBC reservations. However, private colleges separately manage a 35% Management Quota and 5% NRI Quota. Some colleges also have a special SNQ quota for Horanadu and Gadinadu Kannadiga students.
What is the COMEDK UGET 2026 counselling process?
The COMEDK UGET counselling involves: (1) Online registration on comedk.org, (2) Document upload and verification, (3) Choice filling - selecting colleges and branches in order of preference, (4) Seat allotment based on rank and preference, and (5) Reporting to the allotted institution for document verification and fee payment.
What is the eligibility criteria for COMEDK UGET 2026?
Candidates must have passed 10+2 with Physics and Mathematics as compulsory subjects, along with Chemistry/Biology/Computer Science/Electronics. A minimum aggregate of 45% marks (40% for SC/ST candidates from Karnataka) in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics is required. There is no upper age limit for COMEDK UGET.
What is the COMEDK fee structure for different seat types?
COMEDK Merit Seat fees range from Rs 1.2 Lakh to Rs 2.5 Lakh per year. Management Quota fees range from Rs 3 Lakh to Rs 8+ Lakh per year for premium colleges. NRI Quota fees are charged in USD and are significantly higher. All fee structures must be verified on the respective college website and the official COMEDK fee fixation committee order.
Can JEE Main candidates appear for COMEDK UGET 2026?
Yes. Candidates who have appeared in JEE Main 2026 may also be eligible to apply for COMEDK UGET 2026, subject to eligibility criteria. Some COMEDK colleges also accept JEE Main scores for management quota admissions. However, COMEDK merit seats (65%) are filled exclusively using the COMEDK UGET rank.
Is a seat guaranteed after COMEDK counselling allotment?
A COMEDK allotment letter is not a guaranteed admission. You must report to the allotted institution within the specified timeframe, submit original documents, and pay the admission fee to confirm your seat. If you do not report or pay fees within the deadline, your seat may be cancelled.
