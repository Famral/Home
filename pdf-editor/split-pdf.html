Frequently Asked Questions about Splitting PDFs

How can I split a PDF file for free?

Upload your PDF to the Famral Split PDF tool, select the pages you want to extract or the ranges to split by, and click split. Your new files will be ready instantly.

Does splitting a PDF reduce its quality?

No. Splitting a PDF simply extracts the exact original pages without modifying their contents, ensuring 100% of the original quality is preserved.
