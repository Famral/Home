Haryana HSTES B.Tech Counseling Guide

1. Round 1 Online Allotment

The first official off-campus round. Candidates register, pay the fee, and list preferences on techadmissionshry.gov.in. Seats are allocated by JEE Main CRL rank.

2. Round 2 Upgrade & Choice-Filling

For remaining vacancies and candidate upgrades. If allotted a new branch in Round 2, the previous allotment is cancelled automatically, and candidates report to the new institute.

3. Physical / Institute Counseling

After online rounds, colleges announce left-over vacant seats on their websites. Candidates apply directly to individual colleges for merit-based offline admission.

4. All India Quota (AIC)

15% of total sanctioned seats in government and private institutes are open to candidates nationwide. Selection is strictly based on JEE Main AIR CRL rank.

5. Haryana Resident Quota

85% of total seats are reserved for Haryana Residents. It covers sub-reservations like HOGC, SC, DSC, BC-A, BC-B, and EWS. Domicile is verified via PPP (Parivar Pehchan Patra).

TFW Quota Benefits

Tuition Fee Waiver (TFW) offers 5% supernumerary seats in all courses. It waives 100% of the tuition fee for Haryana residents with verified family income below ₹6 Lakhs/annum.

Haryana B.Tech Admission & HSTES Cutoff Insights

Securing admission into Haryana's premier engineering institutes requires understanding the counseling stages, key cutoff trends, and official guidelines. Here is an in-depth breakdown of HSTES admissions.

Top Haryana Engineering Colleges

Haryana is home to several highly reputed government and state university departments that offer excellent B.Tech programs:

  • J.C. Bose UST (YMCA), Faridabad: Known for its strong industry tie-ups and exceptional placement records. Computer Engineering and IT closing ranks are typically very competitive, usually closing within 30,000 to 50,000 CRL ranks.
  • DCRUST, Murthal: Highly popular government university offering diverse B.Tech disciplines. Known for low fee structures and a spacious campus, with CSE closing around 70,000 to 90,000 CRL.
  • UIET Kurukshetra & UIET MDU Rohtak: Premier campus university departments offering comprehensive programs, featuring modern labs and strong research culture. CSE options usually close around 75,000 to 110,000 CRL.

HSTES Cutoff & Seat Allotment Factors

HSTES seat allotment depends on multiple factors that influence closing ranks each year:

  • JEE Main CRL AIR Merit: Admissions are based entirely on your Common Rank List. HSTES does not use category ranks (like OBC/SC/ST category ranks) directly; instead, category seat allocation is done using the JEE Main CRL rank.
  • Choice-Filling Preference Order: Ordering choices strategically is essential. Put highly desired branches at the top (Dream Tier) and always list safe fallback options at the bottom.
  • PPP Verified Quotas: Haryana resident quota benefits (HOGC, BCA, BCB, SC, DSC, EWS, TFW) require strict Parivar Pehchan Patra (PPP) verification. Ensure your category and income are correctly updated in the PPP portal before registration.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is HSTES and how does it conduct Haryana B.Tech counseling?
HSTES (Haryana State Technical Education Society) is the official body that conducts online off-campus counseling for admission to B.E./B.Tech courses in government, government-aided, and private engineering colleges in Haryana. Admissions are strictly based on JEE Main All India Rank (CRL) merit.
Which rank type should I enter in the HSTES College Predictor?
You must enter your JEE Main Common Rank List (CRL) All India Rank. Do not enter category-specific ranks, as the counseling predictor adjusts the search based on historical closing CRL ranks for each category (HOGC, SC, BCA, BCB, EWS).
How does the Haryana Resident quota work in HSTES?
Candidates who have studied in Haryana or hold a valid Haryana Domicile/Resident Certificate are eligible for the Haryana Resident quota (85% of total seats). Under this, seats are sub-reserved for categories like HOGC, SC, DSC, BC-A, BC-B, and EWS. Other state candidates apply under the All India Category (AIC), which comprises 15% of the seats.
What is the role of Parivar Pehchan Patra (PPP) in HSTES counseling?
For Haryana Resident candidates, Parivar Pehchan Patra (PPP) is mandatory to verify category, domicile, and annual family income details for claiming reservation and Tuition Fee Waiver (TFW) benefits.
Are predictions of the HSTES College Predictor 100% accurate?
Predictions are computed using official historical closing ranks from previous years of HSTES counseling. While they indicate your realistic probability, actual cutoffs vary year-on-year based on candidate choices, exam difficulty, and seats. Use it as a robust planning tool.
