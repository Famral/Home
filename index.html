Frequently Asked Questions

What is a college predictor?

A college predictor helps estimate the engineering colleges you may get based on your rank, marks, and choice filling preferences.

How accurate are these predictions?

They provide a strong estimate based on cutoff trends and past counselling data, but final admission depends on actual counselling rounds and official cutoffs.

Which counselling systems are covered?

We provide guidance and predictor links for CUET, REAP, DU, MP DTE, UPTAC, WBJEE, HSTES, ACPC, AKTU, DAP Rajasthan, and JEECUP.

Do I need to pay for using the predictor?

No, Famral’s predictor tools are designed to be free and easy to use for students planning their admissions.
