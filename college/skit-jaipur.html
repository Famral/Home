SKIT Jaipur Rankings & Key Highlights

Established in 2000 and affiliated with Rajasthan Technical University (RTU), Kota, SKIT Jaipur has consistently earned premier recognitions in Rajasthan's technical education landscape:

NAAC A++ Accreditation

Holds the prestigious NAAC A++ grade with a CGPA of 3.67 — the highest possible accreditation from the National Assessment and Accreditation Council, placing SKIT among India's elite private engineering institutions.

NBA Accredited Departments

Core engineering programs including Computer Science & Engineering, Electronics & Communication Engineering, and Mechanical Engineering are accredited by the National Board of Accreditation (NBA).

#1 in Rajasthan by RTU

Ranked #1 engineering college in Rajasthan by Rajasthan Technical University (RTU) Kota for multiple consecutive years, reflecting outstanding academic performance, research output, and placement outcomes.

Academic Departments & Engineering Specializations

SKIT Jaipur hosts industry-aligned academic departments with modern specializations for undergraduate and postgraduate students:

Computer Science & IT

Largest department at SKIT with specializations in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Data Science, IoT, Cyber Security, and Cloud Computing — the most sought after branch for placements.

Electronics & Communication

Covers VLSI Design, Embedded Systems, Signal Processing, Wireless Communications, and Antenna Design — with a strong focus on semiconductor and core engineering career tracks.

Mechanical Engineering

Electrical Engineering

Programs in Power Systems, Control Systems, and Renewable Energy with access to high-voltage equipment and smart grid simulation tools for modern power engineering careers.

Civil Engineering

Covers Structural Engineering, Geotechnical, Transportation, and Environmental Engineering with a fully equipped materials testing lab and survey equipment hub.

Management & MCA

Post-graduate MBA and MCA programs with industry mentoring programs, live project coursework, and cross-departmental electives with engineering departments.

SKIT Jaipur Fee Structure

Tuition fees at SKIT Jaipur are set by the Rajasthan State Level Fee Determination Committee. Below is the comprehensive annual cost breakdown for B.Tech students:

Annual Academic Fees

  • Annual Tuition Fee (REAP Seats): Approximately ₹1,10,000–₹1,20,000 / Year (includes academic tuition, development fees, and university examination charges).
  • Total 4-Year Program Cost: ₹4.6 Lakhs – ₹5.2 Lakhs for the entire B.Tech program (subject to revision by the Rajasthan State Fee Committee each academic year).
  • TFWS (Tuition Fee Waiver Scheme): Candidates with family annual income below ₹8 Lakhs admitted under TFWS pay significantly reduced fees — only basic development and exam charges apply.
  • Management Quota Seats: Fee set independently by SKIT Jaipur. Contact the institute's admission office for the latest management quota fee schedule.

Hostel & Mess Accommodation

  • Nirwana Boys Hostel: 460-seat capacity with single-seated air-cooled rooms. Includes electricity, water supply, 24/7 security, laundry, and recreation facilities.
  • Noran Girls Hostel: 265-seat capacity with double-seated air-cooled rooms. Dedicated warden supervision, separate mess facility, and strict security protocols.
  • Mess Charges (Fooding): Approximately ₹3,000–₹3,500 / Month (cooperative mess system with vegetarian and non-vegetarian meal options).
  • Hostel Caution Deposit: Refundable security deposit required at check-in. Verify exact amount from the SKIT Jaipur hostel administration office.

SKIT Jaipur Admission Process

B.Tech admissions at SKIT Jaipur are primarily conducted through centralized REAP (Rajasthan Engineering Admission Process) counseling, along with a 15% Institutional (Management) Quota filled directly by the institute.

Eligibility & Seat Quota Rules

  • Home State Quota (70%): Reserved for candidates holding a valid Rajasthan Domicile. Allocations are based on JEE Main CRL ranks via REAP counseling.
  • All India Quota (15%): Open to candidates from all states outside Rajasthan. Allocations are based purely on JEE Main CRL ranks through REAP.
  • Institutional (Management) Quota (15%): Filled directly by SKIT Jaipur at the institute level. Candidates must meet minimum eligibility and apply directly to the admission office.
  • Academic Requirements: Minimum 45% aggregate in 10+2 (Class 12th) with Physics and Mathematics as compulsory subjects (40% for reserved categories).

Reporting Document Checklist

  • Allotment Letter: REAP Provisional Seat Allotment Letter (or Institute Allotment Letter for Management Quota candidates).
  • Test Scores: JEE Main CRL scorecard or REAP Merit Certificate.
  • Certificates: Original Domicile, Caste Category (SC/ST/OBC/EWS/MBC), and Income Certificate (for TFWS seats — income below ₹8 Lakhs).
  • School Records: Class 10th Certificate (DOB proof), Class 12th Marks Sheet, Transfer Certificate (TC), and Migration Certificate.

SKIT Jaipur Campus Life & Infrastructure

  • Modern Campus: Situated on a well-developed 13-acre campus at Jagatpura, Jaipur, with state-of-the-art academic blocks, seminar halls, and computing infrastructure.
  • Advanced Laboratories: Over 50 well-equipped labs including AI/ML labs, IoT studios, embedded systems labs, VLSI design labs, and advanced mechanical workshops.

Frequently Asked Questions about SKIT Jaipur

Is SKIT Jaipur a good college for B.Tech CSE?
Yes. SKIT Jaipur is consistently ranked the #1 private engineering college in Rajasthan by RTU Kota for multiple consecutive years. It holds NAAC A++ accreditation (3.67 CGPA) and NBA accreditation for core engineering branches. The CSE department is particularly strong with specializations in AI, Data Science, and IoT, making it highly competitive for placement drives.
How is admission done at SKIT Jaipur for B.Tech?
B.Tech admissions at SKIT Jaipur are conducted through REAP (Rajasthan Engineering Admission Process) based primarily on JEE Main CRL ranks. 85% of seats are filled via REAP centralized counseling, and 15% are Institutional (Management) Quota seats filled directly by the institute. Rajasthan domicile candidates fill 70% of REAP seats while out-of-state candidates fill the remaining 15% All India quota.
What is the annual fee structure at SKIT Jaipur?
The annual tuition fee at SKIT Jaipur is approximately ₹1,10,000–₹1,20,000 per year for B.Tech programs, with the total 4-year program cost ranging between ₹4.6 Lakhs and ₹5.2 Lakhs. Hostel fees are charged separately. Candidates admitted under TFWS (Tuition Fee Waiver Scheme) with family income below ₹8 Lakhs pay significantly reduced fees.
What is the highest placement package at SKIT Jaipur?
The highest CTC package at SKIT Jaipur has reached up to ₹47.32 LPA in recent placement drives, with average packages ranging between ₹4.8 LPA and ₹5.5 LPA. The 2026 placement drive saw 856+ students placed across IT, core engineering, and management sectors.
What are the expected REAP cutoff ranks for SKIT Jaipur CSE?
SKIT Jaipur does not publish a fixed public cutoff rank table. Based on historical REAP merit lists, the expected closing JEE Main CRL rank for CSE (General Category, Rajasthan Domicile) at SKIT Jaipur is generally under 1,80,000 CRL. CSE-AI may close around 2,00,000 CRL, and CSE-Data Science around 2,50,000 CRL. These are estimates — actual allotments depend on the REAP algorithm, your specific rank, and seat availability each year.
Does SKIT Jaipur have hostel facilities?
Yes. SKIT Jaipur has dedicated hostel facilities: Nirwana Boys Hostel with a capacity of 460 seats in single-seated air-cooled rooms, and Noran Girls Hostel with a capacity of 265 seats in double-seated air-cooled rooms. Both hostels include mess facilities, recreation areas, and 24/7 security supervision.
What is SKIT Jaipur's NAAC accreditation grade?
SKIT Jaipur holds a NAAC A++ grade with a CGPA of 3.67, which is the highest possible accreditation grade awarded by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC). Additionally, key engineering departments are NBA accredited and the institute is RTU Kota affiliated and AICTE approved.
What is the management quota process at SKIT Jaipur?
SKIT Jaipur reserves 15% of its total B.Tech intake as Institutional (Management) Quota seats, filled directly at the institute level. Candidates interested in management quota seats must apply directly to SKIT's admission office, meet the minimum eligibility criteria (45% in 10+2 with PCM), and pay the applicable fee as determined by the institute.
